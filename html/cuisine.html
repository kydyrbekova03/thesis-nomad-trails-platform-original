Cultural Celebrations

Manaschi Contests

Manaschi are traditional Kyrgyz epic storytellers who recite the tales of Manas, a legendary hero of Kyrgyz folklore. Manaschi contests are held to preserve and promote this ancient oral tradition, with participants demonstrating their storytelling skills.

Watch More

Nowruz

Nowruz is another important spring festival celebrated in Kyrgyzstan, particularly among the ethnic Kazakh population. It includes rituals, traditional games, and performances showcasing Kyrgyz and Kazakh culture.

Watch More

Eagle Hunting Festival

The Eagle Hunting Festival, known as Salburun in Kyrgyz, celebrates the traditional practice of hunting with trained eagles. This event showcases the skills of eagle hunters and includes competitions and cultural performances.

Watch More

Felt Art and Craft Festivals

Kyrgyzstan is known for its rich tradition of felt-making, and festivals celebrating felt art and crafts are held to showcase the skill and creativity of artisans. These festivals often feature exhibitions, workshops, and demonstrations of traditional felt-making techniques.
